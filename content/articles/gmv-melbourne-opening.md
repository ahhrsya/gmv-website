Global Minang Ventura has officially opened its first Padang Sederhana location in Melbourne, Australia, marking a significant milestone in the brand's international expansion journey.

## A New Chapter in Australia

The Melbourne opening represents years of preparation, market research, and strategic planning. As one of the world's most multicultural cities, Melbourne demands the highest standards of quality, consistency, and authenticity.

## Why Melbourne

Melbourne's diverse, food-sophisticated population and its reputation as a culinary capital made it the ideal Australian market for GMV.
